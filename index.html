WELCOME TO BERU

Beru is a progressive reggio inspired early years school in Bengaluru. At Beru, we strive to nurture a curious, capable and resilient child filled with wonder and knowledge. We strongly believe in providing a supportive and enriching environment to connect parents, educators and the community as a whole.

UNLEASHING UNTAPPED POTENTIAL

Children are competent learners and they can do anything if given the right opportunity, right tools and the right environment. Beru provides just that and more - a school to ignite the natural curiosity of children, a place that values innovation and creativity, and a thriving community that comes together to celebrate learning.

THE BERU WAY

Welcome to your first glimpse of the Beru way of learning. Beru places a special emphasis on empowering future- ready leaders while developing each unique child within our community as lifelong learners. For, we believe the right foundation will nurture the strongest individuals.

The Space

Through the conscious use of space, color, light, displays of children’s art, and attention to aesthetics, nature, and detail, the environment plays a crucial role and is often referred to as ‘the third teacher. Space is designed to encourage encounters, communication, and relationships. The environment is offered to children as a rich context to invite research, with indoor and outdoor Ateliers at the heart of the school.
